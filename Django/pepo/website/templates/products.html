Menstrual Pads

Sanitary pad, also called Kotex or menstrual pad, is a thin pad made from sorbent that absorbs the menstrual fluid during menstruation. These sanitary pads are disposable and are meant for single use only. Sanitary pads are available in different shapes and sizes, with different capacity to soak up for days of heavy and light menstrual bleeding.

Reusable Menstrual Pads

These sanitary pads are made up of many layers of high absorbing materials like cotton, bamboo and hemp which keeps you dry, comfortable and leak-free. They are totally plastic free. Period Underwear

These underwear are designed to look like a regular underwear. These regular looking underpants have multiple layers of microfiber polyester. They soak up menstrual fluid and keeps your skin away from several skin infections and toxic shock syndrome. The outer layer of these underpants are made up of cotton, elastane, lyca and polyamide which makes them leak and water proof and ensuring so stains on clothes.

Menstrual Cups

Menstrual cups are menstrual hygiene product which is shaped like a cup with a bell like stem. Instead of absorbing the flow like a pad or a tampon, these cups catches and collects the discharge. They are highly flexible and are made up of medical grade silicon rubber, latex, or a thermoplastic isomer.

Tampons

Tampons are disposable menstrual product which are shaped like a cylinder. Unlike a sanitary pad, it is placed internally, inside of a vaginal canal and soak up the discharge. It is made up of absorbent cotton or rayon-based material.l

Dysmenorrhea Reliving TENS machine
